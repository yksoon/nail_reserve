Nonoperative spine treatment in Indonesia
Korea English / Bahasa

In this video, a speaker from Hasan Sadikin Hospital, Indonesia, presented on the topic of ' Nonoperative spine treatment in Indonesia'. The speaker lectured that the effectiveness of spinal surgery compared to non-surgical treatments is unclear. In Indonesia, conservative approaches like spinal manipulation, acupuncture, diet, and injections are used for managing spinal pain.
